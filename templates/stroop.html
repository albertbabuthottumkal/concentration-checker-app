Color Switch

The word says "RED" — but it's rendered in cyan ink. Your reading system fires automatically. Override it.

This is the Stroop Effect — two conflicting signals fight for control of your response. Executive function decides which wins.

Rule: Always identify and respond to the ink colour, never the word's meaning.

How to Play

👁️ Identify the ink colour — ignore the word itself

👆 Tap the matching circle
