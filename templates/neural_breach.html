Neural Breach

A sequence memory challenge. Tiles will flash in a pattern — your task is to repeat it exactly.

Watch: Tiles light up one by one in a random sequence.

Repeat: Tap the same tiles, in the same order.

Each correct round adds one more tile to the sequence. How far can you go?

How to Play

🔵 Watch: Tiles will flash in sequence

🧠 Remember: Hold the exact order in memory

👆 Tap: Reproduce the sequence by clicking tiles

⏱️ Timer: 10 seconds once it's your turn

📈 Grows: +1 tile added per round

❌ Game Over: Wrong tile or timeout
