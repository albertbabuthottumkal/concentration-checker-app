Bakhtiyarov Grid

Named after Oleg Bakhtiyarov, Soviet scientist who developed psychonetics in the late 1990s.

Deconcentration: Unlike focused attention, deconcentration trains your perceptive field to process multiple channels simultaneously.

Mechanism: Visual color + auditory number prompts force rapid cross-modal integration — building the capacity for expanded situational awareness.

Applications: Tactical environments, sports, driving, combat sports — anywhere multi-channel monitoring is required.

How to Play

🎨 Visual: Screen shows a color name to target

🔊 Audio: Speaker announces the target number

🎯 Objective: Click the box matching BOTH color AND number

⏱️ Timer: Starts 9 s → shrinks to minimum 2 s per round

📐 Grid scales: 3×3 → 4×4 at score 5 → 5×5 at score 12

❌ Game Over: Wrong box or timeout
